Minesweeper

Instructions

  • The objective of the game is to reveal all the tiles that do not contain a mine.
  • Left click to open/reveal a cell, and right click to place a flag. If a cell is flagged, it will not be opened.
  • The number on the tile signifies the number of neighbouring tiles containing mines.
  • Be careful! If you open a cell containing a mine, you will lose!

Happy Sweeping!
